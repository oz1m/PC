# Random Texts

My friends work at night. Sometimes they don't want to eat, but they always drink water.
If the cook arrives early, then everyone is happy because they can rest.
I ask who is singing, and my dog runs to the door.

Sina todoci mio ni guoka ne noise. Nani daise , sani le no ni guane ni kome; ma, masa cika, sani le ni kome ke guare. Fi, kome ni kini; so, sani le ni sate ke copi; bo, sani le ni pomo ni mate. Mi ni kati pa ke kosa ka keca ni kuta li ko; ta, kanao mio ni kore ki getera.
