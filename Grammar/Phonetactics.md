# Phonotactics
---
## Syllable Structure

All words will be composed by less than 5 syllables, where each syllable is a consonant followed by one or two vowel, where both vowels have to be different (CV) o (CVV): 

- `patao` (Duck): pa-tao, 2 syllables
- `mouse` (Mouse): mou-se, 2 syllables
- `kibodo` (Keyboard): ki-bo-do, 3 syllables
