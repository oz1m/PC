# Phonotactics
---
## Syllable Structure

All words will be composed by less than 5 syllables, where each syllable is a consonant followed by one or two vowel, where both vowels have to be different (CV) o (CVV): 

- `patao` (Duck): pa-to, 2 syllables
- `fonetika` (Phonetic): fo-ne-ti, 3 syllables
- `mouse` (Mouse): mo-se, 2 syllables
- `kibodo` (Keyboard): ki-bo-do, 3 syllables

---
#### Start with vowels
Words can optionally start with any vowel, if that's the case, the vowel alone will be the syllable.

#### End with consonants
Words also can end with 's', describing its plural form. In that case, the 's' will be part of the last syllable.
